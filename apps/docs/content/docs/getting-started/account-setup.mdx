# Overview

Account setup is where you tell the AI Receptionist who you are. This ensures the AI gives your customers correct information about your business from day one.

# Before You Begin

Have your business name, contact email, phone number, and physical address ready.

# Step-by-Step Instructions

### Step 1: Start Setup

Click **Get Started** on the home page.

You should now see the welcome screen.

### Step 2: Select Industry

Click the card that best matches your business type.

Click **Continue**.

### Step 3: Enter Business Details

Fill in:
- Business Name
- Contact Email
- Contact Phone
- Physical Address

Select your timezone from the dropdown.

Click **Launch Workspace**.

# What Happens Next

Your business profile is now ready. 

The AI will use this information when answering customer questions about where you are located or how to contact you.

# Tips

- Use the phone number you want customers to actually call.
- Double-check your timezone so appointments are scheduled correctly.

# Common Problems

**Problem:** The system says my email is invalid.

**Reason:** You might have added a space at the end of the email address.

**Solution:** Check for spaces and make sure you included the "@" symbol.

# Frequently Asked Questions

**Can I change this later?**
Yes. You can update all of these details anytime from the Business Profile page in your dashboard.
