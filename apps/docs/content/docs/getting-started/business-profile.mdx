# Overview

Your business profile contains the core details that the AI Receptionist uses to answer common questions like "Where are you located?" or "What is your phone number?".

# Before You Begin

Have your correct address, contact email, phone number, and social media links ready.

# Step-by-Step Instructions

### Step 1: Open Business Profile

Click **Profile** from the left menu.

You should now see the Business Profile settings.

### Step 2: Update Core Information

Fill in:
- Business Name
- Business Bio / Description
- Support/Contact Email
- Contact Phone Number
- Website Link
- Physical Location Address
- Timezone

### Step 3: Add Review Links

Scroll down to the **Google Maps & Review Settings** section.

Enter your Google Maps link and your Google Review Submission link. 

### Step 4: Add Social Media

Scroll down to the **Social Profile Coordinates** section.

Paste the links to your Facebook, Twitter, Instagram, or LinkedIn pages.

### Step 5: Save Changes

Click **Save Business Profile**.

# What Happens Next

Your profile is immediately updated across the system.

The AI Receptionist will instantly start using this new information to answer customer questions accurately, and can provide your review links or social media handles when asked.
