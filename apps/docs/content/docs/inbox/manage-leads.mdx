# Overview

As your AI receptionist talks to customers, it collects their information. You can view and update this information directly from the Inbox to keep track of your leads.

# Step-by-Step Instructions

### Step 1: Open a Conversation

Click **Inbox** from the left menu and select a conversation from the list.

### Step 2: View the Customer Profile

Look at the right side of the screen to see the **Contact Profile**. This shows the customer's:
- Lead Score
- Estimated Value
- Name, Email, and Phone Number

### Step 3: Update Customer Details

If a customer gets a new phone number, or you want to add a note about them:
1. Click into the box you want to change (like the Phone Number box).
2. Type the new information.
3. Click **Save Profile** at the bottom.

### Step 4: Change Lead Status

You can keep track of where a customer is in your sales process by updating their status. 

1. Find the **Lead Status** dropdown in the Contact Profile.
2. Choose a new status (like "Qualified", "Booked", or "Closed").
3. Click **Save Profile**.

### Step 5: Assign to Staff

If a specific team member needs to handle a conversation, look at the top middle of the screen. Click the dropdown that says "Bot Manager" and select a staff member's name to assign the conversation to them.
