# Overview

The Services menu is where you define exactly what your business offers. The AI receptionist reads this list to tell customers what they can book, how much it costs, and how long it takes.

# Step-by-Step Instructions

### Step 1: Open Services

Click **Services** in the left menu.

### Step 2: Add a Category

If you want to organize your services, you should create a category first.
1. Click **Add Category** in the top right corner.
2. Type a name (like "Haircuts" or "Consultations").
3. Click **Create**.

### Step 3: Add a Service

1. Click **Add Service** in the top right corner.
2. Fill in the details for the service:
   - **Name:** What is the service called?
   - **Description:** A brief explanation of what the service includes.
   - **Price:** How much does it cost? (You can enter 0 if it is free).
   - **Duration:** How many minutes does this service take?
   - **Category:** Select the category it belongs to.
3. Click **Create Service**.

### Step 4: Edit or Delete a Service

If you need to change a price or remove a service:
1. Find the service in your list.
2. Click the **Edit** (pencil) button to change the details, or click the **Delete** (trash can) button to remove it completely.

# What Happens Next

As soon as you add or update a service, the AI receptionist instantly learns the new details and can start offering it to customers who want to book an appointment.
