# Overview

Connecting WhatsApp allows your AI receptionist to answer questions and book appointments directly through the world's most popular messaging app.

# Step-by-Step Instructions

### Step 1: Go to Channels

Click **Channels** in the left menu.

### Step 2: Connect WhatsApp

Locate the **Meta WhatsApp Business** card and click **Connect API**.

### Step 3: Enter Your Details

Fill in the required information for your WhatsApp Business account:
- Integration Label (e.g., "Main WhatsApp Line")
- Phone Number ID
- WhatsApp Business Account ID
- Meta System User Access Token

### Step 4: Save

Click **Save Pipeline**.

# Customize Your AI

Once connected, click **Settings** on the WhatsApp card to adjust how the AI behaves:

- **Enable AI Autopilot:** Turn this on to let the AI reply automatically. Turn it off if you want to reply manually from the Inbox.
- **Conversation Tone:** Choose how the AI sounds (Professional, Friendly, Empathetic, or Casual).
- **Only Business Hours:** Turn this on if you only want the AI to reply during your open hours.
