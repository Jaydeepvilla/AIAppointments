# Overview

Connect your business email address so the AI receptionist can automatically read, process, and reply to customer emails.

# Step-by-Step Instructions

### Step 1: Go to Channels

Click **Channels** in the left menu.

### Step 2: Connect Email

Locate the **SMTP / Gmail / M365 Email** card and click **Connect Host**.

### Step 3: Enter Your Details

Fill in your email server information:
- Integration Label (e.g., "Support Email")
- SMTP Host Server (e.g., smtp.gmail.com)
- SMTP Port (usually 587)
- Username / Account Email
- Password (or App Password)

### Step 4: Save

Click **Save Pipeline**.

# Customize Your AI

Once connected, click **Settings** on the Email card to choose the AI's tone of voice and decide if it should reply to every email automatically.
