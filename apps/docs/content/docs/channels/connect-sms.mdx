# Overview

By connecting your phone number, your AI receptionist can reply to text messages sent by your customers.

# Step-by-Step Instructions

### Step 1: Go to Channels

Click **Channels** in the left menu.

### Step 2: Connect SMS

Locate the **Twilio SMS Core** card and click **Connect Twilio**.

### Step 3: Enter Your Details

Fill in your Twilio account information:
- Integration Label (e.g., "Support Text Line")
- Twilio Account SID
- Twilio Auth Token
- Twilio Phone Number

### Step 4: Save

Click **Save Pipeline**.

# Customize Your AI

Once connected, click **Settings** on the SMS card to adjust how the AI behaves:

- **Enable AI Autopilot:** Turn this on to let the AI reply automatically. Turn it off if you want to reply manually from the Inbox.
- **Conversation Tone:** Choose how the AI sounds (Professional, Friendly, Empathetic, or Casual).
- **Response Delay:** Add a short delay (in seconds) before the AI replies so it feels more natural.
- **Only Business Hours:** Turn this on if you only want the AI to reply during your open hours.
