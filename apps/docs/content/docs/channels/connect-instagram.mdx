# Overview

Connect your Instagram account so the AI receptionist can answer questions from your followers and help them book appointments directly in their DMs.

# Step-by-Step Instructions

### Step 1: Go to Channels

Click **Channels** in the left menu.

### Step 2: Connect Instagram

Locate the **Instagram DM Channel** card and click **Connect Page**.

### Step 3: Enter Your Details

Fill in your account information:
- Integration Label (e.g., "Main Instagram")
- Facebook Page ID
- Instagram Business Account ID
- Facebook Page Access Token

### Step 4: Save

Click **Save Pipeline**.

# Customize Your AI

Once connected, click **Settings** on the Instagram card to adjust the AI's tone of voice and choose if it should reply automatically.
