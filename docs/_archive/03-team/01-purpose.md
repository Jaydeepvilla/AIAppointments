# Purpose: Team Management

## Business Objective
To define the human (or physical resource) capacity of the business. Without staff members, there is no one to perform the services, meaning the AI has no inventory of time to sell to customers.

## Why Module Exists
Not all employees work the same hours. Dr. Smith might work Mondays and Wednesdays, while Dr. Jones works Tuesdays and Thursdays. Furthermore, Dr. Smith might take a week off for vacation. The AI Receptionist needs granular, individual-level schedules to accurately offer time slots to customers without causing double-bookings or scheduling someone who isn't working.

## Problem Solved
- Prevents booking appointments with staff who are on vacation.
- Allows staff to define buffer times (e.g., a massage therapist needing 15 minutes between clients to clean the room).
- Bridges the gap between a "Service Provider" (the person on the calendar) and a "Dashboard User" (the person logging in).

## Success Criteria
- A manager can successfully add a new staff member, set their weekly hours, and the AI immediately begins offering slots for them.
- A staff member can block out next Friday as "Sick Leave", and all available slots for them on that day disappear instantly.

## KPIs
- **Staff Utilization Rate**: Percentage of a staff member's working hours that are successfully booked.
- **Exception Usage**: How frequently staff use the ad-hoc leave/block system (indicating healthy engagement with the tool).
