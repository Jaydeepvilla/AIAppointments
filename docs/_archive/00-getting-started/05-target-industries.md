# Target Industries

## Healthcare & Medical Clinics
- **Business Overview**: High-stress, high-volume environments requiring strict compliance (HIPAA). Services often require pre-qualification.
- **Typical Customers**: Patients seeking routine care, urgent consultations, or specialist referrals.
- **Typical Services**: Consultations, follow-ups, physicals, vaccinations.
- **Common Problems**: High no-show rates, immense phone volume, stringent privacy requirements preventing outsourcing.
- **AI Opportunities**: Triage basic inquiries (hours, location), automate appointment reminders, and handle rescheduling autonomously.
- **Appointment Complexity**: High. Requires matching specific doctors, equipment availability, and enforcing buffer times.
- **Communication Channels**: Voice (primary), SMS (reminders), Web (booking).
- **Special Requirements**: HIPAA compliance, secure data handling, integration with EMR/EHR systems (future).

## Dental Practices
- **Business Overview**: Routine, recurring service models with high lifetime value per customer.
- **Typical Customers**: Families, individuals seeking routine cleanings or emergency dental work.
- **Typical Services**: Cleanings, whitening, consultations, emergency extraction.
- **Common Problems**: Missed calls during procedures, complex rescheduling mechanics.
- **AI Opportunities**: Automated 6-month recall campaigns, handling emergency triage to squeeze patients into cancellations.
- **Appointment Complexity**: Medium to High. Dependent on hygienist vs. dentist availability and specific chair assignments.
- **Communication Channels**: SMS (primary), Voice, Web.
- **Special Requirements**: Handling family bookings (multiple appointments).

## Spas & Salons
- **Business Overview**: Aesthetic and wellness services heavily reliant on visual branding and customer experience.
- **Typical Customers**: Repeat clientele seeking personal care services.
- **Typical Services**: Haircuts, coloring, massages, facials.
- **Common Problems**: Staff managing their own books inefficiently, last-minute cancellations, high variability in service duration based on customer specifics (e.g., hair length).
- **AI Opportunities**: Upselling add-on services during booking, handling massive Instagram/social media DM volume.
- **Appointment Complexity**: High. Dependent on concurrent processing (e.g., color processing time allows for a second haircut appointment).
- **Communication Channels**: Web, Social Media DMs, SMS.
- **Special Requirements**: Granular service durations, specific staff requests by name.

## Law Firms & Legal Consultants
- **Business Overview**: Professional services requiring extreme discretion and lead qualification.
- **Typical Customers**: Individuals or businesses seeking legal advice or representation.
- **Typical Services**: Initial consultations, document review sessions, case strategy meetings.
- **Common Problems**: Wasting highly billable hours on unqualified leads or administrative scheduling tasks.
- **AI Opportunities**: Rigorous lead qualification flows before allowing a booking, autonomous NDA/intake form distribution.
- **Appointment Complexity**: Low to Medium. Standardized consultation blocks, primarily calendar management.
- **Communication Channels**: Web, Voice, Email.
- **Special Requirements**: High data security, attorney-client privilege considerations, payment processing for initial consults.

## Gyms & Fitness Studios
- **Business Overview**: High-volume, community-driven environments.
- **Typical Customers**: Members and prospective members looking to join classes or hire trainers.
- **Typical Services**: Group classes, personal training, facility tours.
- **Common Problems**: Class capacity management, endless inquiries about pricing and schedules.
- **AI Opportunities**: Class waitlist management, membership sales qualification, instant answers to schedule queries.
- **Appointment Complexity**: Medium. Involves capacity constraints (classes) and 1-on-1 availability (trainers).
- **Communication Channels**: Web, Social Media, SMS.
- **Special Requirements**: Capacity limits on single slots, recurring bookings.
