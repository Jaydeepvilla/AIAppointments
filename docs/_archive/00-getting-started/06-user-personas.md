# User Personas

## Business Owner (The Buyer)
- **Goals**: Increase revenue, reduce overhead costs, eliminate operational friction, and maintain oversight of business performance.
- **Pain Points**: Constantly interrupted by administrative tasks, anxious about missed calls equalling lost revenue, frustrated by disjointed software stacks.
- **Permissions**: Full System Access (Owner).
- **Daily Workflow**: Checks high-level dashboards, reviews lead conversion rates, manages billing and top-level configurations.
- **Success Criteria**: Identifies a clear positive ROI within 30 days, reclaims at least 5 hours per week of personal time.

## Manager (The Operator)
- **Goals**: Ensure smooth daily operations, optimize staff utilization, and handle escalated customer issues effectively.
- **Pain Points**: Dealing with last-minute staff call-outs, managing the chaos of double bookings, and training new front-desk staff.
- **Permissions**: Manager (Full operational access, restricted billing/deletion).
- **Daily Workflow**: Manages staff schedules, reviews AI conversation transcripts for quality assurance, intervenes in escalated conversations, and adjusts service parameters.
- **Success Criteria**: Zero double bookings, high staff utilization, and seamless shift management.

## Staff / Practitioner (The Service Provider)
- **Goals**: Focus entirely on delivering their expertise (dentistry, styling, consulting) without worrying about calendar management.
- **Pain Points**: Showing up to gaps in the schedule, not knowing a client's specific requests before the appointment, and dealing with scheduling conflicts.
- **Permissions**: Staff (Access restricted to own calendar and assigned customers).
- **Daily Workflow**: Checks their daily schedule on their phone, views client notes prior to service, blocks out specific times for breaks or emergencies.
- **Success Criteria**: Arrives to a full, accurate, and manageable calendar every day with zero manual effort.

## Customer (The End User)
- **Goals**: Book a service, get an answer to a question, or modify an existing appointment as quickly and easily as possible.
- **Pain Points**: Being put on hold, waiting 24 hours for an email reply, navigating clunky web forms, or dealing with restrictive business hours.
- **Permissions**: None (External interface only).
- **Daily Workflow**: Interacts with the AI widget via the business website or SMS to ask questions or find a suitable time slot.
- **Success Criteria**: Achieves their goal (booking, answering, rescheduling) in under 2 minutes, regardless of the time of day.

## Administrator (Internal Platform Admin)
- **Goals**: Ensure platform stability, monitor tenant health, and manage global system configurations.
- **Pain Points**: Debugging tenant-specific configuration issues, managing platform-wide database migrations.
- **Permissions**: Super Admin (Internal staff only).
- **Daily Workflow**: Monitors system logs, manages feature flags, assists support with escalated technical tickets.
- **Success Criteria**: 99.99% uptime, rapid resolution of tenant-level technical bugs.

## Support (Internal Customer Success)
- **Goals**: Help businesses onboard successfully, configure their AI properly, and resolve usage questions.
- **Pain Points**: Deciphering complex, tenant-specific AI logic or schedule conflicts without clear visibility.
- **Permissions**: Support / Read-Only Admin.
- **Daily Workflow**: Answers tickets, guides users through knowledge base ingestion, reviews logs to explain AI behavior to clients.
- **Success Criteria**: High CSAT score, low time-to-resolution, and successful onboarding of new tenants within 48 hours.
