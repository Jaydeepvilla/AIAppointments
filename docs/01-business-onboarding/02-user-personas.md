# User Personas: Business Onboarding

## Business Owner

### Goals
- Create an account for their business to start automating receptionist tasks.
- Understand the pricing structure and select an appropriate plan.
- Quickly configure the basics so the AI can start working immediately.

### Responsibilities
- Provide accurate legal and operational information (Business Name, Address, Timezone).
- Enter valid payment credentials to activate the subscription.
- Verify ownership via email or SSO.

### Permissions
- Assigned the `Owner` role automatically upon successful organization creation.
- Has unrestricted access to all onboarding steps and subsequent dashboard configurations.

### Pain Points
- High anxiety regarding software complexity. If the onboarding requires immediate API keys or complex logic, they will churn.
- Fear of the AI making mistakes; they need reassurance during onboarding that they have control.
- Frustration with long, multi-page forms asking for non-essential information.
